PPC(Pay Per Click), also known as CPC(Cost per Click) is an online advertising model where the advertiser pays a publisher for every click. PPC is the most widely used advertising model for all paid ads. PPC ads are broadly classified into Search Engine Advertising and Display Ads.

Search Engine Ads.

Search Engine Ads or SEM is where an advertiser pays search engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc to show their ads above or below the organic search results. A company will generally have multiple products or services. And then there are many different search terms people use to search for these services and products. Ranking on the first page organically with SEO for all of these possible search terms is not possible for a brand. This is where Search Engine Marketing comes in and makes it possible for even smaller brands to compete with large companies and get highly qualified traffic to their websites. SEM compliments SEO to get a wider coverage on Search Engines.

Frequently asked questions about Paid Search & Google Ads

What is paid search and how is it different from SEO?
Paid search (Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, Bing) puts your website at the top of results for a chosen keyword in exchange for a per-click fee. SEO earns the same placement organically over months. The two work best together: paid for fast pipeline, SEO for compounding free traffic.
How much does Google Ads cost in India?
Most SMB campaigns spend ₹30,000 to ₹3,00,000 per month on media, with management fees typically 15-20% of spend or a flat retainer from ₹25,000/month. We size budget based on cost-per-click in your category, target volume and revenue goals.
How long until Google Ads delivers leads?
Search ads can deliver qualified leads inside 48-72 hours of launch once tracking is live. The first 3-4 weeks are spent learning: tightening match types, building negative keyword lists, and optimising landing pages and bids until cost-per-acquisition stabilises.
Which platforms do you run paid ads on?
Google Search and Performance Max, Microsoft Ads, Meta (Facebook + Instagram), LinkedIn Ads, YouTube Ads, X Ads and Amazon Ads. We pick channels by buyer intent, target audience and unit economics, not by familiarity.
How do you measure ROI on paid search?
We measure cost-per-acquisition (CPA), return on ad spend (ROAS), customer lifetime value (LTV), assisted conversions, view-through conversions and lead quality. Reports use Looker Studio dashboards with full-funnel attribution.
Do you write ad copy and create landing pages?
Yes. We write Responsive Search Ads, headlines, descriptions and creative assets, plus design and develop conversion-optimised landing pages with A/B-tested variants for your priority campaigns.
