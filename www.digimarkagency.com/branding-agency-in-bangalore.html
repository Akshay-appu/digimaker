BONUS: Local SEO Checklist for Bangalore Businesses

Want to supercharge your Google visibility with branding? Here's a checklist you can follow:

Bangalore Local SEO Branding Checklist

  • Claim and verify your Google Business Profile for an accurate address (Ex: Koramangala 4th block)
  • Use consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across web platforms
  • Incorporate high-quality photos with brand styling
  • Request and respond to Google reviews regularly (mention location)
  • Include location keywords in website schema and meta tags
  • Build backlinks from local Bangalore newspapers, blogs or forums
  • List on niche directories like Sulekha, JustDial, and Clutch (with brand story)
