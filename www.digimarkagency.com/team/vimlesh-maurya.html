About Vimlesh

Head of SEO at DigiMark Agency Bangalore

Vimlesh Maurya leads SEO strategy and execution at DigiMark Agency in Bangalore. He has 11+ years of hands-on search experience across technical SEO, local SEO, ecommerce SEO, enterprise SEO and AI-search readiness (GEO/AEO) for businesses in Bangalore and across India.

His work spans early-stage startups, growth-stage SaaS companies, e-commerce brands, real-estate developers, healthcare networks and B2B firms. The thread running through every engagement: senior in-house specialists, transparent reporting through Google Search Console, GA4 and Looker Studio, and accountability to commercial KPIs — non-branded traffic, demo requests, store enquiries and signed contracts — rather than vanity rankings.

Vimlesh personally reviews every SEO programme DigiMark publishes on the website, including the flagship SEO Company in Bangalore service page, the Local SEO services page and the Technical SEO services page.

Approach

How Vimlesh runs an SEO programme

The approach is built around transparency and measurable outcomes, not promises. Every engagement starts with a discovery audit (technical, content, local, link), maps to a 90-day roadmap with clear weekly tickets, runs through a monthly strategy call and quarterly business review, and reports through a live Looker Studio dashboard that the client owns.

There are no opaque "black-box" deliverables. Everything — from the keyword tracker to the technical-issue queue, content calendar, internal-link map and GBP action log — is visible to the client at all times.
