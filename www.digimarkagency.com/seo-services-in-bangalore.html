Technical SEO services in Bangalore

Technical SEO is the foundation everything else stands on. You can publish excellent content and earn strong links, but if Google cannot crawl your pages efficiently, render them correctly, or trust their structure, that investment underperforms. Our technical work makes your site fast, accessible and unambiguous to search engines.

What this covers

  • Crawl & indexation control — XML sitemaps, robots directives, canonical logic, and fixing index bloat or orphaned pages so only the right URLs compete.
  • Core Web Vitals & page speed — LCP, INP and CLS improvements through image, script and render-path optimisation, on mobile and desktop.
  • Site architecture & internal linking — a logical hierarchy and link structure that distributes authority to the pages that matter.
  • Structured data — Organization, LocalBusiness, Product, FAQ and Breadcrumb schema for richer, more eligible search results.
  • JavaScript & rendering — ensuring content on JS-heavy and single-page sites is actually visible to crawlers.
  • Migrations & redirects — protecting rankings during redesigns, replatforming and URL changes.

Technical SEO pairs naturally with a full SEO audit — we diagnose first, then fix in priority order.

Local SEO services in Bangalore

Local SEO targets the searches that happen with intent and a location attached — “near me” queries, neighbourhood searches, and the Google Map Pack that sits above the regular results. For clinics, retailers, restaurants, real-estate and multi-location service businesses across Bangalore, this is often where the highest-converting traffic comes from.

What this covers

  • Google Business Profile optimisation — categories, services, attributes, posts, photos and Q&A managed to win Map Pack visibility.
  • Local landing pages — genuinely useful, distinct pages for the areas and services you serve — not thin doorway copies.
  • Citations & NAP consistency — accurate name, address and phone data across directories that search engines cross-check.
  • Reviews & reputation — ethical review-generation workflows and response management.
  • Local content & links — relevance signals tied to Bangalore neighbourhoods and communities.

Ecommerce SEO services in Bangalore

Ecommerce SEO has a different shape to lead-gen SEO. Catalogues create thousands of URLs, faceted navigation can spawn near-infinite duplicates, and the money lives on category and product pages rather than blog posts. Our ecommerce work is built to scale rankings and protect conversion, on Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento and custom stacks.

What this covers

  • Category & collection SEO — the highest-value commercial pages, optimised for head and mid-tail buying queries.
  • Product-page optimisation — titles, descriptions, Product and Review schema, and internal links from relevant categories.
  • Faceted navigation & crawl budget — controlling filter/sort parameter URLs so crawl budget is spent on pages that should rank.
  • Duplicate & thin content — canonicalisation and consolidation across variants and out-of-stock handling.
  • Conversion-focused SEO — aligning rankings with on-page elements that turn visits into sales.

SEO audit services in Bangalore

An SEO audit is the diagnosis that should precede any retainer. Rather than guessing, we establish exactly what is limiting the site today and what will move the needle — so budget goes to the highest-impact fixes first, not a generic checklist.

What an audit examines

  • Technical health — crawlability, indexation, Core Web Vitals, mobile usability and structured data.
  • On-page & content — intent alignment, cannibalisation, content gaps and internal linking.
  • Off-page — backlink profile quality, toxic-link risk and competitor link gaps.
  • Competitive positioning — where rivals out-rank you and why, mapped to realistic opportunities.

You receive a prioritised action plan with expected impact — usable whether you work with us or not.

SEO services in Bangalore — FAQs

What SEO services does DigiMark offer in Bangalore?

DigiMark offers technical SEO, local SEO, ecommerce SEO and SEO audits in Bangalore, supported by on-page SEO, content and ethical authority building. Each engagement is scoped to your site, your competition and your commercial goals after a free audit.

Is there a difference between SEO services and search engine optimization services?

No — “search engine optimization services” and “SEO services” are the same thing; SEO is simply the abbreviation. Both describe the work of improving a website’s visibility in organic search through technical, on-page, content and authority work.

What is technical SEO and why does it matter?

Technical SEO is the work that lets search engines crawl, render and index your site reliably — site architecture, Core Web Vitals, indexation control, structured data and internal linking. Without it, strong content and links underperform because pages cannot be accessed or trusted.

How is local SEO different from regular SEO?

Local SEO targets location-based intent — “near me” searches and the Google Map Pack — using Google Business Profile optimisation, local landing pages, citations and reviews. Regular SEO targets organic rankings across all of search, regardless of the searcher’s location.

Do you handle ecommerce SEO for online stores?

Yes. Ecommerce SEO covers category, product and collection-page optimisation, faceted-navigation and crawl-budget handling, product schema, and conversion-focused on-page work for platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce and Magento.

What is included in an SEO audit?

An SEO audit reviews technical health, indexation, Core Web Vitals, on-page optimisation, content gaps, backlink profile and competitor positioning, then prioritises fixes by expected impact. It is the recommended starting point before any retainer.

How long do SEO services take to show results?

Most programmes show measurable ranking and traffic movement within 3–4 months, with meaningful business impact between months 6 and 12. Timelines depend on competition, the site’s current condition and content velocity — not on shortcuts.
