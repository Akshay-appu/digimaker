CRO Services in Bangalore — Quick answers

What is Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)?

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is the structured process of increasing the percentage of website visitors who take a desired action — purchase, signup, lead form, demo, install — using research, A/B testing, UX analysis and iterative experimentation. CRO grows revenue from your existing traffic without raising ad spend.

Which is the best CRO agency in Bangalore?

DigiMark Agency is one of the best CRO agencies in Bangalore with 11+ years of experience running experimentation programmes for ecommerce, SaaS, B2B, EdTech and lead-gen brands. Our CRO experts pair quantitative analytics with qualitative research (heatmaps, session replays, user interviews) to build a prioritised testing roadmap.

DigiMark’s CRO plans start at ₹49,999 for a one-time audit, ₹99,999/month for the Growth CRO programme, and ₹1,49,999/month for Enterprise CRO with full experimentation, dev support and personalisation.

How long does CRO take to show results?

Most CRO programmes show measurable conversion lift within 60–90 days. The first 30 days are research and audit; weeks 4–12 are the first round of A/B tests. Statistically significant winners typically need 2–4 weeks of traffic per test. Compounded over 6–9 months, well-run CRO programmes often deliver 1.5x–3x conversion improvement.

What does a CRO expert in Bangalore actually do?

A CRO expert audits your funnel using GA4, heatmaps and session replays, identifies friction points, prioritises hypotheses by ICE/PIE scoring, designs A/B and multivariate tests, ships experiments via tools like VWO, Optimizely or AB Tasty, analyses results for statistical significance, and ships winning variants to production. The goal: grow conversion, AOV, signup rate and qualified leads from existing traffic.

Each framework comes with industry-specific benchmarks and a tested-test backlog.

What CRO tools do you use?

We use VWO, Optimizely, AB Tasty, Microsoft Clarity, Hotjar, FullStory, Mixpanel, Amplitude, GA4, Looker Studio, UserTesting and Maze. Tool selection depends on traffic volume, stack and budget — we recommend the right combination for your business rather than a one-size-fits-all setup.

How do you measure CRO success?

We measure CRO success on primary metrics (conversion rate, revenue per visitor, signup rate, qualified leads), secondary metrics (AOV, bounce rate, scroll depth, time-on-page) and downstream metrics (LTV, CAC payback, retention). Every test report includes statistical significance, confidence interval, sample size, and segment-level lift.
